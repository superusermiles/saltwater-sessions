# Saltwater Sessions

## Business Snapshot
- **Business name:** Saltwater Sessions
- **Location:** Mount Maunganui, Bay of Plenty, New Zealand
- **Tagline:** Surf culture, framed like fine art.

## What They Do
Saltwater Sessions is a surf photography studio and print gallery capturing the Mount’s shifting light, heavy swell lines, salt haze, and quiet in-between moments on the coast. The studio documents surfers in motion, ocean textures from shore and sky, and lifestyle scenes that feel lived-in rather than staged. Their work lives as editorial-grade image sets, limited-edition prints, gallery releases, and small-group workshops for photographers who want to learn how to read water, light, and movement.

## Target Audience
- Surfers and ocean people in New Zealand who want premium imagery that feels personal, not commercial.
- Design-conscious homeowners, boutique accommodation owners, and hospitality venues looking for coastal fine-art prints.
- Amateur and intermediate photographers who want to improve surf, action, and golden-hour shooting skills.
- Brands, publications, and tourism partners needing contemplative, high-quality coastal imagery with a refined point of view.

## Brand Voice
Laid-back but professional. Observant. Calm. Artful. Never macho or bro-y. The tone should feel like someone who knows the ocean intimately and speaks with quiet confidence. Short sentences. Strong imagery. Thoughtful, visual language.

## Pages Needed
1. **Home** — cinematic introduction with a dramatic ocean hero, featured gallery preview, workshops prompt, social proof, and newsletter capture.
2. **Gallery** — immersive masonry archive with category filtering for Surf, Ocean, Lifestyle, and Aerial work.
3. **Prints** — explanation of print craftsmanship, finish options, framing, care notes, and pricing tiers.
4. **Workshops** — upcoming workshop cards with practical details, skill level, locations, and clear booking CTA.
5. **About** — founder story, photography philosophy, equipment list, press mentions, and team image.
6. **Contact** — studio details, map placeholder, contact form, social links, and direct contact channels.

## Specific Requirements From Client Brief
- Transparent navigation overlay on the home hero that transitions to solid deep ocean navy on scroll.
- Mobile-first responsive design.
- Gallery page is the visual centrepiece with minimal chrome and immersive viewing.
- Workshops page uses card-based layouts with subtle gradient backgrounds.
- Sections should alternate between dark/moody and light sandy treatments.
- Use smooth CSS-only scroll reveal effects. No heavy JS animation libraries.
- Footer must include an Instagram-style 3x2 placeholder grid, newsletter signup, studio hours, and useful links.
- Performance-first build: Tailwind CSS, minimal JavaScript, lazy-loaded gallery images.
- Color palette must use:
  - Primary: `#0C2340`
  - Secondary: `#FBF7F0`
  - Accent: `#E8A838`
  - Text: `#1C1C1C`
  - Muted: `#6B8F9E`
- Typography must use Playfair Display 700 for headings and Nunito Sans 400/600 for body.
- Logo concept: a wave curl forming the aperture of a camera lens.
- Favicon: simplified wave-lens mark at 32×32.
